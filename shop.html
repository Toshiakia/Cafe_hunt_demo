Welcome To Rake Café!

Welcome to Rake Café! Since our founding in 1840, we've strived to offer a warm and welcoming environment right in the city's heart. Rake Café is more than just a place for great coffee; it's a vibrant community brought together by a shared passion for quality and sustainability. Our founder, Malaka, has always believed in the power of inclusivity and the beauty of diversity, ensuring every visit is a memorable experience.

At Rake Café,

we cater to everyone. Families can enjoy our child-friendly zones, the LGBT community will find a safe and welcoming space, and for those needing a spot to work, we offer high-speed WiFi and comfortable seating. Our amenities, including accessible restrooms and cozy reading nooks, ensure your comfort.

Join us

for a coffee experience where every cup tells a story, and every visit feels like coming home.
